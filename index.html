Breakthrough Video


I made the video below for the 2020 Breakthrough Junior Challenge, a student video-making challenge. Participants from around the world make a three-minute educational video in the life sciences, physics, or math (or COVID-19 for 2020). My video was in the COVID-19 category and talks about how we can use virus mutations to track how Coronavirus spreads.

While I was a novice at using professional-grading editing software such as Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro, I have a great deal of experience using Microsoft PowerPoint, so I made the animations for the video almost exclusively in PowerPoint and edited the video in PowerPoint and Windows Video Editor. I learned a great deal about viruses, mutations, and tracking virus genomes using computing, something I hope to explore more in the future!
